## Key Features Overview

### 1. Dashboard
Upon logging in, you will see the Dashboard. This is your mission control center.
- **Metrics:** View real-time counts of users, active records, and recent activity.
- **Navigation:** Use the sidebar on the left to switch between different modules.

### 2. User Management
As an Admin, you can manage who has access to your system.
- **Add Users:** Click "New User" to invite team members. They will receive an email to set their password.
- **Roles:** Assign roles (Admin, User) to control permissions.
- **Status:** You can deactivate users if they leave your organization without deleting their historical data.

### 3. Data Management
The core of the application allows you to manage your business data (Items/Records).
- **Create:** Use the "Add New" button to input data.
- **Uploads:** Attach documents or images directly to records.
- **Search:** Use the search bar above the table to find specific records quickly.

### 4. Settings
Access settings via the user menu in the top right corner.
- **Profile:** Update your name and avatar.
- **Security:** Change your password.
- **App Config:** Update your business name or email notification preferences (requires Admin role).
